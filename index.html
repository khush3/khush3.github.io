Biography

I am a graduate student at the Robotics Institute, Carnegie Mellon University (CMU) working with professor Aviral Kumar on offline reinforcement learning. I am interested in working at the intersection of machine learning (ML) and robotics.

Previously, I worked as a research associate at professor Howie Choset’s Biorobotics Lab on skill learning and snake robots. During my undergrad, I spent time working at professor Shital Chiddarwar’s IvLabs, building a stair climbing robot and an assistive robot. I spent the summer of 2020 working in professor David Held’s R-PAD Lab as a part of the Robotics Institute Summer Scholars program remotely.

Beyond research, I find joy in music, running, biking, and spending time outdoors.
